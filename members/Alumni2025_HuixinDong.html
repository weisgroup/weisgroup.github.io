Huixin Dong Class of 2020. Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)
Email

Huixin Dong is a PhD candidate at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) under the supervision of Prof. Wei Wang. He also collaborate with Prof. Jie Xiong and Prof. Lili Qiu at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) on GPS sensing.

Research Interest

Backscatter Network; GPS Sensing
